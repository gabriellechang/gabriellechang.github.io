About

Laundry Care is a resource devoted to providing you the best laundry tips. We have gathered expert information from detergent companies, chemists, and people like you! Our guides are perfect for you if you are doing your laundry for the first time (without your parent's help!) or if you are wondering how to get that peanut butter stain off your silk blouse.
