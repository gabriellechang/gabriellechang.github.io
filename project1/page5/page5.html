I am the 
    
poor white

                           ,
    fooled and pushed apart,
                   I am the 
    
Negro bearing 
  slavery's scars.

                          I am the 
    
red man driven 
  from the land,

    
I am the immigrant clutching 
  the hope I seek--

    
        
                    And finding only the same old stupid plan
                    Of dog eat dog, of mighty crush the weak.
